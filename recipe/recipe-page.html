Creamy Butter Chicken

Living in Los Angeles exposed me to several different cultures and their cuisines. However it wasn't till my first trip to Canada that I tried Indian cuisine. Since then it has easily become one of my all time favorites.

Photo Credit: Butter Chicken Recipe by Cooking Classy

Ingredients

Chicken marinade:

  • 28 oz boneless and skinless chicken breasts chopped into chunks
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 1 1/2 tbsp minced garlic
  • 1 tbsp minced ginger
  • 2 tbsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp of salt

    • For the sauce:

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp butter & 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 1 1/2 tbsp garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp ginger, minced
  • 1 1/2 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • 14 oz crushed tomatoes
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1/2 tsp kasoori methi

    • Make the marinade

    1. Combine Ingredients for the chicken marinade and let chicken marinade for at least 30 minutes

    Cook chicken with sauce

    1. Cook chicken in an oiled pan until brown on both sides/fully cooked
    2. Add 1 tbsp butter and 1 tbsp oil to pan. Fry 1 large chopped onion until it browns
    3. Add 1 1/2 tbsp of minced garlic and 1 tbsp of minced ginger. Stir until fragrant
    4. Add 1 tbsp of ground coriander, 1 1/2 tbsp of cumin and 1 1/2 tbsp of garam masala. Stir till fragrant
    5. Add 14 oz of crushed tomatoes, 1 tsp of chili powder and 1 1/4 tsp of salt. Simmer for 10-15 minutes until color deepens with occasional stirring
    6. Blend sauce in a mixer until smooth
    7. Add 1 cup of heavy cream, 1 tbsp of sugar and 1/2 tsp of kasoori methi. Let cook for an additional 10 minutes
